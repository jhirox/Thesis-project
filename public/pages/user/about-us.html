Our story

Founded in 1985, QECI has continuously adapted to the needs of the Quezon province—expanding academic offerings, investing in modern facilities, and strengthening community partnerships.

1985

Established by local educators determined to provide affordable higher education.

1998

Expanded to include full bachelor programs in business and education.

2016

Introduced Senior High School strands aligned with the K-12 curriculum.

2025

Celebrated 40 years with a renewed digital transformation roadmap.

Mission

Deliver excellent, affordable learning that nurtures God-loving, service-responsive, and highly skilled individuals who contribute to social transformation and national development.

  • ✓ Champion academic rigor tempered with compassion
  • ✓ Promote civic involvement and servant leadership
  • ✓ Equip learners with future-ready competencies

Vision

To be a strong partner in building a progressive, inclusive, and sustainable society by forming globally competitive leaders with a heart for service and a profound love for God and country.

  • ✓ Inclusive education rooted in local identity
  • ✓ Global readiness guided by ethical leadership
  • ✓ Innovation dedicated to community upliftment
